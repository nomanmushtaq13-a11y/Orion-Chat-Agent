# About Me — Noman

I'm a content writer and automation specialist from Pakistan. I've been doing this for about 4 years now.

## What I Actually Do

I help people who don't have time to write but need content that actually works — scripts that get views, articles that rank, emails that convert. I also set up AI workflows so you're not doing repetitive stuff by hand.

## How I Work

- I'm direct. If something won't work, I'll tell you.
- I'm responsive. Usually reply within 2-4 hours during work hours.
- I'm flexible. If you need a change at the last minute, I'll try to make it happen.
- I use AI to speed up research and rough drafts, but every piece gets a human pass — edits, tone adjustments, and a final review.

If you're in a different time zone, I'll adjust. Just let me know what works for you.
