## Common Questions — Answered Honestly

**Q: What kind of content do you write?**
A: Mostly YouTube scripts, blog articles, website copy, and email sequences. If it involves words and strategy, I've probably done it.

**Q: Do you optimize for SEO?**
A: Yes. I do keyword research, structure articles properly, write meta descriptions, and keep readability in mind. But I won't stuff keywords — I write for humans first.

**Q: Can you write in Urdu or Hindi?**
A: Only English, professionally. For Urdu/Hindi content, I partner with native speakers to ensure quality.

**Q: What do you need from me to get started?**
A: Just the topic, your target audience, and any reference material you have. If you're not sure about the direction, I can help figure it out — I'll ask the right questions.

**Q: How many revisions do I get?**
A: Two rounds are included. Need more? I charge half the project rate for additional revisions.

**Q: What if I don't like the work?**
A: First, I'll revise it until you're happy. If we really can't make it work, we can discuss a partial refund or scope change. I want you to be satisfied, not just "okay with it."

**Q: Do you use AI?**
A: I use AI tools for research and getting rough drafts going. But every piece is edited, rewritten, and fine-tuned by me — by hand. My clients get the final product, not a ChatGPT copy-paste.

**Q: Can you rewrite content I already have?**
A: Yes. Rewriting and content refreshes are available at 60% of the new content rate.

**Q: Are you available for calls?**
A: Definitely. I prefer video calls for new projects — it's faster than typing everything out and I get a better sense of what you need.

**Q: What's the fastest you can deliver?**
A: For rush orders (50% extra), I can deliver a 1000-word article in 12 hours. For scripts, about 24 hours.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for long-term work?**
A: Yes. If we're working together regularly, I'll give you better rates than one-off projects. Let's talk about what you need.
