## Common Questions

Q: What platforms do you write for?
A: YouTube, blogs, websites, LinkedIn, email newsletters, product pages

Q: Do you provide SEO-optimized content?
A: Yes. All content includes keyword research, proper headings, meta descriptions, and readability optimization.

Q: Can you write in languages other than English?
A: Professional English only. For Urdu/Hindi content, I collaborate with native speakers.

Q: What information do you need from me to start?
A: Topic/theme, target audience, preferred tone, any reference materials, keywords if available.

Q: Do you offer revisions?
A: Yes, up to 2 rounds of revisions included. Additional revisions charged at 50% of project rate.

Q: How do you handle deadlines?
A: I deliver on time or communicate early if any delays arise. Rush orders (+50% fee) available for urgent work.

Q: Can you rewrite existing content?
A: Yes, rewriting and content refresh services available at 60% of new content rates.

Q: Do you use AI in your work?
A: I use AI tools to enhance research and efficiency, but every piece is human-reviewed, edited, and customized to your brand voice.

Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the work?
A: I offer revisions until you're happy. If it's still not working, we can discuss partial refund or scope adjustment.
