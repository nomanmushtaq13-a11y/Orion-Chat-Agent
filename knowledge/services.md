# Delivery Process

Honestly, the way I work is pretty simple:

1. We talk about what you need — I ask questions, you share your vision.
2. I put together an outline or plan. You approve it.
3. I write the first draft within the agreed timeline.
4. You review it. If something's off, I fix it — up to 2 revisions included.
5. Final delivery with source files.

No fuss, no bureaucracy. Just clear communication and quality work.
